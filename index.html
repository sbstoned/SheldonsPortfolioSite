About Me

I'm Sheldon Sharp, a Full Sail University Game Development graduate who loves building cool things with code. From video games and mobile apps to graphics programming and business automation, I'm always looking for the next challenge to tackle.


I work with C++, C#, Unreal Engine, Unity, Vulkan, Flutter, Kotlin, SQL, and Python, creating everything from custom software solutions to interactive experiences. My passion is taking an idea from a sketch on a whiteboard and turning it into something people can actually use.


I firmly believe that if a task is repetitive, it should be automated. If a game mechanic is fun, it can always be made more fun. And if there's a bug hiding somewhere in the code, I'll eventually find it usually at 2 AM.
