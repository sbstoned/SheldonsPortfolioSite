Utahni

A 2D side-scrolling action game developed in Unreal Engine. Players can swap between two unique characters to solve challenges, defeat enemies, discover hidden keys, and progress through increasingly difficult levels.

Features a custom procedural generation system, enemy encounters, character switching mechanics, collectible items, platforming gameplay, and combat systems.

Technologies Used:

Unreal Engine 5 • C++ • Blueprints • AI Systems • Procedural Generation • Gameplay Programming
