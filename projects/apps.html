NDS Barcode Scanner

A mobile barcode scanning application designed for distribution centers that still rely on older legacy ERP systems. The app helps streamline inventory updates by allowing users to scan product barcodes and generate data that can be used with warehouse and inventory workflows.

This project focuses on improving speed, accuracy, and efficiency for inventory-related tasks while supporting business systems that may not have modern scanning tools built in.
